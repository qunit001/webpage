For Universities & Training Institutes

Bring an industry-run cyber security program to your campus

The biggest gap in Indian cyber education is faculty who can teach current, market-ready skills. We close that gap — as a productized offering any university or college can license, not just a one-off engagement.

Faculty Development Programs (FDP)

Structured, hands-on upskilling so your faculty can teach offensive security, GRC and AI security with the same tooling industry uses — delivered on a repeatable, certifiable basis.

Semester-wise Module Licensing

Ready-to-teach modules mapped semester by semester — lecture material, labs and assessments — that a department can license and run without building content from scratch.

CTF Platform & Competition Hosting

A hosted Capture-the-Flag platform for continuous practice, inter-college competitions and skill benchmarking — turning theory into measurable hands-on ability.

Cyber Range & Hands-on Labs

Cloud-based lab environments and a cyber range for realistic attack/defend exercises, so students graduate having actually done the work, not just read about it.

External Evaluation & Certification

Independent, industry-graded assessment of student capability and a project/capstone evaluation service — giving your program credibility employers trust.

Corporate & Professional Training

The same practitioner-led training delivered to enterprise security teams and working professionals, from awareness to advanced red-team tradecraft.
