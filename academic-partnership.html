Program Structure

B.Tech Cyber Security — 184 Credits, 8 Semesters

An industry-run degree built in progressive stages — from secure programming foundations to advanced offensive operations, AI-driven defense, and a full-time industry capstone.

Foundations (Sem 1–2)

Secure programming, data structures, computer architecture, OS hardening and Python for security.

Core Security (Sem 3–4)

Ethical hacking & VAPT, network defense, digital forensics & incident response, and IoT/OT security.

Advanced & Offensive (Sem 5–6)

Cryptography, malware analysis & reverse engineering, red teaming, zero trust and AI/ML for cyber defense.

Specialisation (Sem 7)

Domain & open electives spanning cloud, SOC, threat intelligence, blockchain and quantum — plus an innovation capstone.

Industry Immersion

Structured internships every semester and a full-time final-semester industry internship.

Certification Pathways

Semester-aligned preparation for CompTIA, Security+, CEH, eJPT/PNPT, CHFI and OSCP-level credentials.
